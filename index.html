Project Description

Overview

Topic:


We approach this project with a belief in better motorbike trips. Every Vietnamese person has to commute every day but to be honest, it is one of the most boring daily activities. Not only that, but it is also even inconvenient when we do not know how to navigate around the city and have to use GPS. Unlike driving a car where you can check your phone without serious risks like robbing and losing balance on a motorbike, we always have to face those pain if we decide to use GPS. Therefore, our helmet will be the solution to every motorbike driver’s problems.

With our smart helmet, it will change the behavior of people in Viet Nam while riding their own bikes on the road. Each trip travels with a motorbike will become less boring, feel more excited. It also helps the rider to concentrate more on the traffic, stay away from distraction while riding such as upcoming phone calls, text messages,... the helmet can create a brand new experience for people to feel more enjoyable, convenient and also makes their trip become safer than before.

Motivation:

In normal daily life, we can not deny that the phone plays an important role in our life because it can handle many works such as receiving calls, emails,.., and also guiding people from this place to their final destination. But using phones while riding or driving in common is usually dangerous and even can cause unexpected accidents. That is where our idea came from, also the motivation for this project: to prevent accidents, increase the safety of riding motorbikes on the road.

Landscape:

So far, there is some similar system or product that is also integrated into the speedometer of the motorbike. I think with this our product because our idea is more convenient, you don’t have to look at the road you are riding on and look down at the speedometer to see the notification or who is calling you at that time. With our idea, you just have to focus on the road, all of the notification will appear as well but it still maintains the view for the rider to focus while riding.

Detailed Description

Aims:

This project aim towards the rider’s riding experience. Ride on a motorbike is not fun if you are riding alone, it is just full fill your desire. With our helmet, your trip will become more exciting, make it more colorful and also safer with the help of advanced technology. To full fill that aim, there are 2 main goals that need to be achieved is the convenience for riders while riding on a road and the concentration when you are riding, make the riding experience become safer, more excited and create a brand new world with the help of advanced technology.

About the first goal, while riding, you will feel really bored such as while you are riding in the city but in the rush hour, you just sit on your motorbike just move forward slowly because of the traffic jam. Our helmet can make you feel more exciting with the integrated speaker and projector inside the helmet, which will raise your experience into the new level, make your boring trip from your home to the office into a more excited, more interesting trip.

Into the second goal, the smart helmet will have an integrated device, which will present a new notification of who is calling or texting you, show the way from your place to your destination while you are riding without using your phone. To solve this problem, the system integrated into the helmet has to have an interface that is user-friendly and also easy to use. all of the above just to have the rider feel more confident, more convenient while riding on the road. Also, the helmet has to have sensors that will alert riders about the dangerous situations for the rider, also remind the rider to focus on the road if they have many notifications or continuous notification shows on the screen of the helmet system. All of that helps the riders to focus more on the road, increase the concentration while riding on a motorbike.

    Tools and Technologies:


    Augmented Reality (AR): We use this technology to connect real life with the virtual world so that the user can still have awareness about the surrounding when looking at the interface of the helmet.

    Holographic Display: This display is similar to the modern car. There will be a mini projector to display the image onto the glass surface. Because this will be less noticeable than if there is a high contrast screen, the user can clearly keep their eyes on the road.

    Surround sound record and playback: The helmet will have a microphone outside of the helmet to capture the noise of the environment and mimic that through the built-in surround stereo headphone. This feature will always turn on and not be adjustable, we make this decision to force the driver to keep awareness of the traffic.

    Bluetooth: This is the way to connect the helmet with your phone to play the music and display the google maps from your own personal device. This way the helmet does not need to connect to wifi, all wifi related functions can be processed on the user’s phone and it’s data.

    Google Glass, Maps and Assistant: We will try to buy the license from google to use their technologies and apply that on our product. We use their technology to control the whole system, apply the AR function and use GPS. Because we use Google Maps, it will show the direction and crowded roads to help the driver.

    Emergency Alert: When there is an acidence, the helmet will use a microwave to check whether the user’s still have consciousness. It will automatically call the medical service if the user has passed out.If the user still has their awareness, the helmet will ask whether they need it or not and wait for the user to answer.

    Voice Control: To make the driver keep both of their hands on the handle bar, we have to find a hands-free method for the user to interact with the helmet. We found out that a voice control system is the best option. There will be a microphone attached to the mouth cover part of the helmet, and through that microphone the user can command the helmet as they want.

    Recyclable material: Nowadays, electronic waste disposal is a big issue in the world. Electronic devices keep being improved and released every year, so everyone has to throw away the old ones to get the new design. By using recyclable materials, in future if we release the newer version, we can still recycle the one helmet.

    Build-in Dash Cam: This is for recording the user’s journey and commute. If the user loves to have a road trip but every now and then, buying an actual action camera such as a GoPro is too costly and wasteful, so they can just use the camera that is located on the forehead part. This also works as a dash cam which is alway recording our commute just in case that the user got into an accident.

    Testing:

    We will have some physical tests for a normal helmet. Firstly, we will undertake the impact test, the reason behind this test is the safety for the drivers who wear it. We have to make sure our product is durable enough to protect the users. Next will be the water proof test, because there will be rainy days, the helmet should resist rain water to protect the electric components inside. For the user interaction test, we will look for 10 to 20 volunteers. We will give 3 helmets to 3 people from that group to use for 3 days each time. After 3 days, those 3 helmets will be given to the 3 others from the same group. Then, we will ask them to give a report during the end of their tests. Each time they give us a report, we will have a day to adjust and fix the bugs. This will take 3, 4 or more weeks to undertake the testing, this will be dependent on the bugs and difficulties that we can find during the testing. We will finish the test if 3 groups in the row are fully satisfied with the result.
